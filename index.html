Frequently Asked Questions

Which areas in Dallas do you serve?

We provide mobile services across the entire Dallas area, with primary focus on Highland Park, University Park, Preston Hollow, Lakewood, and Uptown.

How long does a detailing take?

Depending on the package, our service typically takes between 2 to 4.5 hours. We ensure every inch of your vehicle is perfected.

What is your rain policy?

If rain is forecasted, we offer free rescheduling. If you have a covered garage at your home or office, we can usually proceed as planned.

Our premium cleans start at $149 for sedan maintenance and scale up based on vehicle size and the intensity of the detailing required.
