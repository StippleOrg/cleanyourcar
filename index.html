Common Questions

Do I need to provide a water source?

No. Our mobile units are fully self-contained with their own purified water supply and whisper-quiet power generators.

What happens if it rains?

We monitor the Dallas weather closely. If rain is expected, we offer a 24-hour rescheduling window or can work in your home garage/parking structure.

Is my area covered?

We primarily serve the Greater Dallas area, including Downtown, Uptown (75201/75202), Highland Park, University Park, and Lakewood. Contact us for locations further out.
